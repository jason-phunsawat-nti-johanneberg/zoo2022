Food for you AND the animals!

At ZooFood, we offer a variety of delicious and unique dishes inspired by the animals within the Zoo and from around the world. Our menu features something for everyone, including vegetarian and gluten-free options. Come dine with us and enjoy great food and amazing animals! None of the food hurts the animals meaning that you can feed them yourself!

The animals loves the interaction!

ZooFood is home to a wide variety of animals, including lions, monkeys, flamingos, and more. Our animal exhibits are designed to give you an up-close and personal look at these amazing creatures. Come visit and learn about the wonderful world of animals at ZooFood and observe the animals eating from your very own hands!
